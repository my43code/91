Diploma pathway

Early Childhood Care and Education

Hands-on, community-focused preparation for teaching and childcare.

Program Requirements

  • Completed application form with personal statement.
  • Certified academic transcripts and diploma or certificate copies.
  • Three recent 2x2 ID photos and a character reference letter.
  • Medical clearance and proof of financial support where applicable.

Full payment instructions and supporting document templates appear after you choose to view this section.

Bachelor’s degree pathway

Program Requirements for Bachelor’s Degree

Advanced preparation across accounting, ICT, business, health and more.

Program Requirements

  • Completed bachelor application form and two character references.
  • Certified transcripts showing GPA 2.0+ with no grades below C.
  • Two recent ID photos plus a valid medical certificate.
  • Proof of financial support and payment acknowledgement when applicable.
